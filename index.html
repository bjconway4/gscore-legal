G-Score Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how G-Score ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

When you use G-Score, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email address, name, and profile picture from your authentication provider.
  • Photos and Videos: Images and videos you upload to score your Guinness pints. These are used solely for generating your G-Score.
  • Scores and Activity: Your G-Scores, pub tags, and leaderboard rankings.
  • Location Data: If you grant permission, we use your location to find nearby pubs. This data is not stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • Scoring: Your uploaded photos/videos are sent to OpenAI's vision API to analyze and score your Guinness split. Images are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored by OpenAI.
  • Leaderboards: Your username and scores may appear on public leaderboards within the app.
  • Pub Discovery: We use Google Maps to help you find and tag pubs.
  • App Improvement: Aggregated, anonymized data may be used to improve the scoring algorithm and app experience.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your uploaded images are stored in secure cloud storage associated with your account.

Third-Party Services

G-Score uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database storage
  • OpenAI: Image analysis for scoring (images are processed but not retained)
  • Google Maps: Pub search and location services
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In: Authentication

Data Retention

Your account data and scores are retained as long as you maintain an active account. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Children's Privacy

G-Score is intended for users who are of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the legal drinking age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
